SKII.tech

SKII.tech is an augmented reality heads-up display for improving the alpine skiing experience. Public data from OpenSnowMap was used to virtually map out ski trails, as well as to provide navigation functionalities like finding shortest paths and all paths to a chosen destination. Developed as part of a team of three for nwHacks 2020.
